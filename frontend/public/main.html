How to Check Your CCTV

  1. Log in to your account to save your data.
  2. If you want to update your database, upload the image on this page.
  3. If you want to find your CCTV image, go to the playback page.
  4. Use the filtering feature to set the date and time.
  5. The image for the specified date and time will be displayed.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why is my image not showing up after uploading?

Make sure the uploaded file is in a supported format, such as PNG or JPG, and does not exceed the maximum allowed size (1000 MB). If the problem persists, try refreshing the page or contacting our support team.

Can I upload more than one image at a time?

Currently, the upload feature only supports one image per session. If you want to upload another image, please repeat the upload process.

How do I use the filter feature to rotate the image to the desired time?

After the image has been uploaded, click the Playback button to view the image that has been adjusted with the selected filter.
